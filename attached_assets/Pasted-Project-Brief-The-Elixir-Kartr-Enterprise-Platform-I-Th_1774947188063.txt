Project Brief: The Elixir Kartr Enterprise Platform

I. The Core Vision
We are building a new digital home for Elixir Kartr, a luxury AI/ML strategy firm. This is a complete departure from a personal portfolio. We are creating a "Digital Headquarters" that feels intelligent, stable, and high-end.

II. Design Language
	- Aesthetic: Dark Mode luxury. Deep charcoal backgrounds with "Glassmorphism" layers.
	- The Hero: The Gold Mandala (attached) is the heart of the brand.
	- Motion: Use Framer Motion for smooth, "liquid" transitions. Nothing should snap; everything should glide.

III. Technical Architecture & Solutions
The site must showcase our deep engineering expertise across five specific industry "Verticals." Please build these as clean, interactive components using the following data:
1. Fitness & Sport 
	- Workout Form Analysis: Video Intelligence for pose and keypoint detection.
	- Personalized Training: Multimodal recommendations (Video + Audio + User Data).
	- Wearable Analytics: Audio and sensor data processing.
2. Media & Entertainment 
	- Content Intelligence: NLP and Multimodal recommendation engines.
	- Video Analysis: Automated classification and summarization.
	- 3D Generative AI: Image-to-3D and Text-to-3D context creation.
3. Finance (Wall Street) 
	- Sentiment Analysis: Multimodal processing of Audio and Text.
	- Market Microstructure: Advanced Reinforcement Learning (RL) models.
	- Portfolio Optimization: RL-driven asset management.
4. Real Estate 
	- Property Valuation: Combining Computer Vision with Tabular data.
	- Virtual Property Tours: Video Intelligence integrated with 3D AI.
	- Maintenance Prediction: Computer Vision paired with IoT sensor data.
5. Manufacturing 
	- Quality Control: Computer Vision for defect detection.
	- Predictive Maintenance: Time-series forecasting for machinery.
	- Strategic Optimization: RL for Production and Supply Chain forecasting.

IV. Step-by-Step Implementation
	1. Framework: Setup a clean React.js/Next.js environment.
	2. 3D Element: Integrate a Three.js interactive Mandala in the Hero section that reacts to the user's cursor.
	3. Copywriting: Change all "I" references to "We" or "Elixir Kartr" to establish a corporate presence.
	4. The "In-Person" Hook: Create a prominent CTA for "Request an In-Person Consult." We want to move conversations away from LinkedIn and into real-world meetings. (like mail sent me)

V. Final Deliverable
A high-performance, responsive web application that proves Elixir Kartr is the gold standard in AI/ML Engineering and Strategic Thinking.
